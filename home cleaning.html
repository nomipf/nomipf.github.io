Home Cleaning

Getting help with home cleaning does not have to be complicated or particularly expensive. You choose how often you want help with the cleaning and to what extent. Some just want help with parts of the house from time to time while others want complete house cleaning every week.

Since a cleaning company works in your own home, it is important to choose someone you feel comfortable with and who has the right insurance. You as a customer can always trust us because all our services and workers are fully insured. We have a collective agreement with Fora, AFA Försäkring and Länsförsäkring for both our workers and our business.


Regular Home Cleaning

We offer various options of cleaning subscriptions for both private and corporate customers. The cleaning can be done daily, every week, every other week or every four weeks depending on what your needs are. We tailor and design the cleaning arrangement together with you. We offer good standard arrangements for our cleaning subscriptions and services, but you as a customer always have the opportunity to influence what your cleaning arrangement should look like. We care about our customers, which is why we always carry out our work with a little extra care and accuracy. Our customers deserve to come home to a clean and newly cleaned home.

Cleaning and breaks when subscribing to a cleaning subscription

We guarantee cleaning during all 12 months of the year. Should you go on holiday or want a break during the summer, no problem, with us you as a customer can choose to have a cleaning break of up to a month.

You only pay for what you use

With us, you only pay for what we deliver, no extra surcharges on time. The agreed time is what we will perform the promised job on. In the event of deviations such as severe dirt in the home or extra services, a deviation in time may be added, however, up to 30 minutes to avoid high costs for the customer.
