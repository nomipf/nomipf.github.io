Knowledgeable staff

One of the most common views / complaints when it comes to properties is the smell of too strong cleaning agents. One reason for this is that the cleaning agents used are very concentrated. In addition, it happens that untrained cleaners use the wrong dosage, which gives an excessively strong odor.

An untrained cleaner may think that an extra strong odor is a sign that cleaning has taken place. Some people try to hide the smell of something else, which can make it difficult for the property owner / manager to pay attention to an underlying problem with e.g. ventilation or moisture problems.

Too strong a scent is the wrong way to "show the customer" that a cleaning has taken place. In most cases, it also does not get cleaner just because you use more cleaning agents.
