Relocation Assistance

Just the thought of moving takes its toll. But don't worry, we'll help you when it's time. When you move to another city or within your city, our moving assistance always includes transport, moving boxes and insurance. In addition, we can help you with packing / unpacking and assembly / disassembly.

About the Service

To transport your things to your new address, our professional moving team offers help. With other things related to your move, such as packing and moving cleaning, we can also help you.

The practical

Our experienced moving staff ensures that your belongings arrive at your new address on the day of the move. You can either leave your key with us in advance or you can stay at home and let us in.

You as a customer can also trust us because all our services and workers are fully insured. We have suspension agreements with the Property Employees' Union and collective agreements with Forums, AFA Insurance and Länsförsäkring for both our workers and our operations.

How to book

Write to us and we will contact you within one working day. No matter what you include, you always get a fixed price from us.
