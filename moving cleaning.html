Moving Cleaning

Let us take care of the moving cleaning so that you can put the energy into the new home yourself!

In order for it to feel completely safe to hire us, we provide a guarantee for the removal cleaning and immediately rectify any deficiencies at the time of inspection. We follow current guidelines and ensure that the cleaning meets all requirements for being approved by the new owner or tenant.

The cleaning is carried out according to a carefully planned work schedule to guarantee a good result every time and the price is set with the living space as a starting point. We strive to always have a good customer service and offer quick feedback when needed.

We have cleaning materials with us so you as our customer do not have to think about buying the necessary cleaning materials for the moving cleaning but can concentrate on your new home and the move.
