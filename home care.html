Home Care

It is difficult to keep everything in top condition on houses and villas. There are a lot of small fixes everywhere and each season has its must-haves for the building to feel good. We come with the help! We know everything that is required and can help you so that the house and garden feel good for many years.

Does the door to the kitchen sound too much, do you have broken light bulbs that you cannot reach, has the handle stuck to the garage, full gutters or a window that needs to be insulated? Maybe your store has become overcrowded with rubbish to be driven away and thrown away? No problem for us!

You as a customer can also trust us because all our services and workers are fully insured. We have a collective agreement with Kommunal fackförbund, personal insurance with Fora and AFA Försäkring and Företagsförsäkring with Länsförsäkring.
